Cultural Resonance · Design Language v0.1

One voice. Two breaths.

A token system for Rosewood Sand Hill's Cultural Resonance product. Same colour, same family, same line — guest surfaces breathe wide and quiet; staff surfaces compress dense and decisional. Asymmetry expressed in a single attribute toggle.

03 · Surfaces in use

Same engine. Same renderer rules. Different density.

Both panels render from one Cultural Reasoning call. The staff cockpit applies the dense renderer (WHY visible, availability visible, alternatives visible). The guest tablet applies the restraint renderer — one line, no reasoning, no alternatives.

04 · The Memory Artifact

Where the system speaks at full voice.

Rosewood Sand Hill · remembers

Sand Hill remembers a Saturday that began in mist. The fog sat low over the oaks that morning — the kind of stillness that doesn't ask to be broken. You took your tea on the terrace while the garden was still yours alone, and the pu-erh carried something of West Lake across the Pacific.

那个周六的雾气，和杭州清晨的一样轻。你们在雾里重新找到了"回家"的节奏——不是探索，不是抵达，只是回到彼此身边。这是 Sand Hill 记得的。

Two weeks after departure · in the place's voice
